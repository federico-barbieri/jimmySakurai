A tribute
to
Jimmy Sakurai's

OBSESSION

If you've never heard of Akio Sakurai

it's because that name doesn't exist.

For the past 30 years, he has been Jimmy Sakurai,

The most thorough imitator of Jimmy Page.

Akio was born in Japan, but his mind was reborn

after listening to Led Zeppelin.

From then onwards, Akio would aspire

to perform exactly like the band.

Same outfit.

Same instruments.

Same sound system.

There was no room for mistakes.

Only for improvements.
